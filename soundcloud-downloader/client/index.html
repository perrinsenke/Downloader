App Info & Guidelines

What you can download:

  • Public tracks, albums, playlists, and artist profiles on SoundCloud
  • Any standard Spotify song, album, playlist, or artist profile

What will fail:

  • Private or deleted tracks
  • SoundCloud Go+ exclusive tracks
  • Region-blocked content

Note: If you download a playlist containing private tracks, the app will simply skip them and download the rest!
